# Xordon - Ultimate Agentic SaaS Walkthrough

Welcome to Xordon, the most advanced, ultra-premium agentic coding platform. This walkthrough will guide you through the key features and modules implemented during this project.

## 1. Reputation & Reviews
*   **AI Review Response**: Automatically generate context-aware replies to customer reviews.
*   **Facebook Integration**: Direct integration with Facebook Graph API to post replies.
*   **Review Widgets**: Customizable widgets to display your best reviews on your website.

## 2. Loyalty & Rewards Program
*   **Points System**: Fully configurable points-to-currency ratios.
*   **Customer Search**: Real-time search for customers to manual point adjustments.
*   **Transaction History**: Detailed audit log of every point earned or redeemed.
*   **Rewards Library**: Create custom rewards like fixed discounts, percentage off, or free products.

## 3. Marketing Suite
*   **Social Planner**: Schedule and manage posts across Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
*   **AI Content Refinement**: Use AI to polish your social media captions for maximum engagement.
*   **QR Codes**: Dynamic QR code generator for marketing campaigns.
*   **Affiliate Management**: Manage your own affiliate team and track referrals.

## 4. Webinar Studio
*   **Ultra Low Latency Streaming**: Built-in WebRTC based streaming directly in the browser.
*   **Interactive Rooms**: Real-time chat, reactions (heart, zap, smile), and screen sharing.
*   **Registrant Management**: Track who signed up and manage attendees in real-time.
*   **Evergreen Webinars**: Automated triggers for passive income generation.

## 5. Payments & E-commerce
*   **Text2Pay**: Send payment links directly to customers via SMS.
*   **Unified Checkout**: High-conversion checkout pages for any product.
*   **Subscription Management**: Handle recurring billing and interval-based plans.
*   **Revenue Attribution**: Track exactly where your sales are coming from.

## 6. Field Service & GPS
*   **Map Integration**: Real-time GPS tracking for field technicians.
*   **Dispatch Board**: Visual scheduling for service-based businesses.
*   **Mobile App (PWA)**: Full offline support and installable mobile experience.

## 7. AI Agents & Automations
*   **Chat Agents**: Custom AI personas for customer support and sales.
*   **Call Agents**: Voice-based agents integrated with SignalWire for outbound dialing.
*   **Workflow Builder**: Visual drag-and-drop automation builder.
