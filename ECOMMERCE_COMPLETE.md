## üöÄ HOW TO USE

### Inventory Management
1. Navigate to `/ecommerce/inventory`
2. Click "Add Stock" to add inventory for a product
3. Select product and warehouse
4. Set quantities and reorder points
5. Save and track stock levels in real-time

### Coupons
1. Navigate to `/ecommerce/coupons`
2. Click "Create Coupon"
3. Set code, name, type, and value
4. Configure usage limits and validity dates
5. Activate and share with customers

### Shipping
1. Navigate to `/ecommerce/shipping`
2. Click "Add Method"
3. Configure rate type and pricing
4. Set delivery time estimates
5. Activate for customer checkout

### Collections
1. Navigate to `/ecommerce/collections`
2. Click "New Collection"
3. Add name, description, and image
4. Organize products into collections
5. Use for seasonal promotions or categories

### Store Integration
1. Navigate to `/ecommerce`
2. Click "Connect Store"
3. Select platform and enter credentials
4. Sync products and orders
5. Monitor abandoned carts and recover sales
