## Key Features Across All Templates

### Functional Elements
✅ **Conditional Logic Ready** - Step-based forms support progressive disclosure
✅ **Date/Time Pickers** - Built-in date, time, and datetime field types
✅ **File Uploads** - Photo and document upload capabilities
✅ **Mobile-Friendly** - Responsive design settings included
✅ **Digital Signatures** - Text-based signature fields for approvals
✅ **Validation Rules** - Min/max values, required fields, pattern matching
✅ **Multi-Step Progress** - Progress bars and step indicators
✅ **Diverse Field Types** - Text, email, tel, select, radio, checkbox, textarea, file, date, time, number

### Design Variations
- Each template has unique color schemes (not generic)
- Modern theme settings
- Different form types: single_page, multi_step
- Industry-specific categorization
- Comprehensive vs. quick-capture options

### Business Categories
- **Lead Generation** - 3 comprehensive forms (General, Lawn Care, Painting)
- **Operations** - 4 forms (Timesheet, Service Report, Invoice, Job Application)
- **Service Requests** - 1 form (Maintenance)
- **Feedback** - 2 forms (Review, Complaint)
- **Marketing** - 4 forms (Newsletter, Referral, 2 Opt-ins)
- **Sales** - 1 form (Order)

## Usage Instructions

All templates are now available in the form builder and can be:
1. Selected from the template gallery
2. Customized for specific business needs
3. Embedded on websites
4. Shared via links
5. Integrated with existing workflows

Each template includes:
- Pre-configured fields with appropriate types
- Validation rules where needed
- Logical step progression (for multi-step forms)
- Industry-appropriate language and options
- Modern color schemes
- Mobile-responsive settings
